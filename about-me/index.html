About me

October 21, 2022 · Serhat Giydiren

Hi, I’m Serhat.

I am on a career break after more than two decades of hard work.

I worked for Microsoft, Garanti BBVA, Akbank and Getir.

I have a deep interest in Mathematics, Theoretical Computer Science, Discrete Optimization, and Large-Scale Distributed Systems. I also enjoy competitive programming and have participated in numerous coding competitions.

It’s an interesting story that I started blogging to keep notes just for myself. The notes were generally related to tech interviews because, around that time, I was interviewed by Google and Meta (Facebook). After a while, however, I started receiving emails and messages from people thanking me for some of the articles on my blog. Sometimes, they also asked for advice for their upcoming interviews.

Because I had notable tech interview experience across all stages (phone/technical screening, on-site, virtual on-site interviews, hiring committee phases, offer negotiations, etc.), I decided to add more articles related to that field.

Feel free to email me if you’d like me to write an article related to tech interviews.
